About Me

I am a PhD student (2021-) at School of Computer Science and Engineering of Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Cong Gao. Prior to NTU, I received my Master's degree (2016-2019) and Bachelor's degree (2012-2016) at the School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University (WHU). In WHU, my research was advised by Prof. Zhenzhong Chen at the Lab. of Intelligent Information Processing (IIP).

I work on Spatio-temporal data mining, Information Retrieval (IR), and Machine Learning (ML).
